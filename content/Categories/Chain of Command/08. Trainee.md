**Consular Trainee is the Last Low Rank of the Pathway.**

To become a Trainee you must attend and Pass 2 [[Lightsaber Training|Lightsaber Trainings]], 2 [[Seminar|Seminars]], 1 [[Healing Lecture]] and 1 [[Healing Patrol]], and be a [[09. Prospect|Consular Prospect]] for 3 days or more.

(Can skip this CoolDown by simply attending 20+ events as [[09. Prospect]])

Once this is all done you may use our [#rank-request](https://discord.com/channels/869439124682203137/1406008763890274364) Channel to request your rank up to trainee using the Proof.

---

The Quota for Trainees is attending 3 Events.
