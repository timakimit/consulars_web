**Knowledge Keeper is the First Staff Team Rank.**

To gain this rank you must be a Consular Healer who has Passed his Knowledge Keeper Trials.

The main responsibility of Knowledge Keepers is hosting the bulk of the Pathways events.

---

The Quota of Knowledge Keepers is Hosting 2 events a Week.
