**Consular Healer is the First Middle Rank of the Pathway.**

To gain this Rank you must pass your Advanced Jedi Knight Trials.  
Jedi Consulars are able to Co-host Standard Events.

Once you become a Jedi Consular you gain access to the Force Ability [[Mass heal ability|Mass Heal]].
