**The Rank of Sage is given to the Senior High Ranks of the Pathway.**

To gain this rank you must be a currently serving member of the High Rank team and pass a your Sage Trials.

Sages of the Consular Pathway are eligible to take on their Master Trials.

---

Sages Deal with Minor reports within the pathway, Voting, Ranking and logging.  
Their Quota is Hosting 3 events a Week.
