This article lists all the discord rules, that members of the discord server should maintain.
> [!tip] Tip:
> If have any queries regarding the rules stated below, please reach out to a Sage or above.
# Rules:
## Rule Ⅰ:

Be respectful to all members of the Order. This varies on how much someone can tolerate, but slandering and outright attempts to trigger someone are not allowed.

## Rule Ⅱ:

Ignoring warnings from Moderators or HR will lead to a temporary mute, or even a kick/ban.

## Rule Ⅲ:

Threats of any kind to any members within this discord or in-game will not be tolerated.

## Rule Ⅳ:

Spam in any chat is not permitted. For the sake of having access to them, do not misuse the channels.

## Rule Ⅴ:

You may not advertise or recruit our members in this Discord Server or in-game. This will result in a blacklist.

## Rule Ⅵ:

The use of slurs is not permitted as it will lead to a mute. Persistence results in removal from the pathway or even from the Order.

## Rule Ⅶ:

Any NSFW content will lead to a ban, depending on severity. If it is anything children related, you will be reported to Discord along with an unappealable blacklist from the server and group.

## Rule Ⅷ:

IP-grabbing links will result in a ban. This is also breaching [Discord ToS](https://discord.com/guidelines "https://discord.com/guidelines") so it will be reported depending on the severity.

## Rule Ⅸ:

Do not mute/ban skip with an alt(s), or leave the server and rejoin to remove the mute role. You will be met with a more severe punishment.

## Rule Ⅹ:

Phishing, doxxing, raids, etc. will be handled by a permanent ban and reported to Discord.

## Rule Ⅺ:

Don't start arguments you have no intention of resolving. We ask you to take it to your DMs.
