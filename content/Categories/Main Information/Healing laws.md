This article lists all the healing laws that members of TJO should maintain.
# **The Healing Laws of the Jedi Order**

- Jedi are **not** permitted to: heal Jedi engaged in a duel until that Jedi has been disengaged from combat for at least **THREE seconds**, or if their opponent(s) is dead. Jedi Consulars Healers+, TG Vanguard (When Guarding), TG while being at a disadvantage as if it is fighting a hostile using rage or being outnumbered and Jedi Council+ are **exempt from this rule.**

- Jedi may **not** heal anyone that is **beyond the white line.**

- Jedi are **restricted** to only healing **Jedi and Hostiles on Sith Planets**, **Healing Hostiles on Coruscant and/or Sith Order Members** is **forbidden.**

###### _Note: Mass-Heal does not affect Members of **The** Sith Order or Hostiles on Coruscant_

- **Consular Healers+** are urged to not use **Mass-Heal near** ongoing **1v1s against Jedi.**

- **Consular Healer**+ are exempt from the 3 sec rule when combat healing Jedi in the midst of combat.

# Possible Punishments:

For Non-Consular Pathway members, the possible punishment to any of these offences is merely a **Warning.**

Otherwise, for members of the Consular Pathway, possible punishment can range from: **A Warning, JJs, and/or a Pathway Strike.** In most **serious offences,** members of the Consular Pathway could obtain **2 strikes.**
