Advanced Jedi Knight (AJK) Trials are trials that let you get AJK (Consular Healer) rank. To complete these trials, you must fill out the document linked below, and then send it in [#event-request](https://discord.com/channels/869439124682203137/1405904497380954237) channel, using proper format, pinned in the channel. Ambassador+ may host these trials.
