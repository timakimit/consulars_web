# Questions:

- **Q: How do I become a Jedi Consular within the main group?**
    
    - _A:_ To become a Jedi Consular (AJK) in the main group, you must achieve the rank of Consular Trainee and pass your Trials of Advanced Knighthood.
---
- **Q: How can I get mass heal in-game?**
    
    - _A:_ To receive mass heal, you must achieve the rank of Consular Healer. More information can be found within the Ranking Guide. The keybind for massheal is N.
---
- **Q: How do I log my activity?**
    
    - _A:_ Every time you hop onto Coruscant or Korriban to aid combatants, run the “/clockin” command in the Consular droid grounds. When you leave the game, run the “/clockout” command in the same channel. You may also use plugin for roblox website, called "RoPro" to log your activity.
---
- **Q: What Institutes can I join as a Jedi Consular?**
    
    - _A_: You are able to join the Librarian’s Assembly, Jedi Researchers & Jedi Academy.
---
- **Q: Who is able to host my Advanced Jedi Knight Trials?**
    
    - _A_: Those that are Ambassador and above and have the qualification.
---
- **Q: I wish to begin my vote my Knowledge Keeper. Who should I contact?**
    
    - _A_: Contact a Sage+
---
- **Q: I witnessed a member of the pathway acting out of conduct. Who should I talk to about this?**
    
    - _A:_ Contact a Sage+, if the member in question is in the Consular staff team, contact a Consular Master+
