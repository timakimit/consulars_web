Consular Practice Inspections are hosted on a seldom schedule. The purpose of these inspections is to assess the capabilities and skills of each Consular. Inspections may be Joint Path, where all three pathways are gathered, or Internal, involving solely the Consulars. **Those that are the rank of Consular Master with the awareness of the Council of First Knowledge may host Practice Inspections.**

**Practice Inspections May be hosted in the Archive hallway.**
