On occasion we may be summoned by a body such as the Jedi Task Force to raid an enemy map such as Korriban. With our unique mass heal ability, we help serve as combat medics during their raids. Raid Assistances may be held by a Sage+, and may replace a Healing Patrol for rank requirements.

**Raid Assistance may be hosted on Korriban or Coruscant when assisting in a Raid.**
