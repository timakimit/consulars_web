A Initiation Exam will focus on testing new Pledges on the basic knowledge needed to become a part of the Consular Pathway.  
Testing the attendees knowledge of the Archives, Healing Laws, Consular Formation and their skills in combat.

Initiations may be hosted by Ambassador+

---

**Initiation Exams may be hosted in different areas of the temple based on the segment:**  
The _**Trial of Knowledge**_ may be hosted in the Archives.  
The _**Trial of the Body**_ may be hosted in the combat mats.
