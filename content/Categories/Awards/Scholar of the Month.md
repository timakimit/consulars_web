The Scholar of the Month title is awarded to the highest performing member within CoFK, chosen as a candidate by the Caretaker then considered by CoFK Oversight . SoTM is announced at the start of each Month the winner of this title is announced by the Oversight in the corresponding CoFK server. Anyone within CoFK is eligible to become SoTM.

The award for earning Scholar of the Month are as follows:
- Personal “Scholar of the Month” (shiny) title within the Consulars Discord.
    
- Jedi High Council Endorsement
