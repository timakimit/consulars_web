**Endorsements are ratifications of exceptional performance or service awarded by the Higher Ranks of the Consular pathway.**

**There are 3 levels of endorsement you may earn, each one will be covered alongside how to earn one.**

---

**Pathway Endorsements:**

Being the lowest in value, these endorsements are typically given out by Junior staff members to award exceptional performance in common settings.

- Win an unfair duel during a Consular event.
    
- Win a The Chosen One during a Consular event. (TCO must have 5+ participants)
    
- Win a FFA in a Consular event **5 times** (FFA must have 5+ participants)
    
- Win an FFA in a Consular event (must have 10+ participants)
    
- Survive the Survival Training in a Healing Lecture.
    
- Pass a Seminar event without any strikes, **3 Times.**
    
- Be labeled as MVP in a healing patrol with 7+ attendees. (excluding hosts)
    
- Earn 2nd or 3rd place for Consular of the Week.
    
- Earn 4th or 5th place for Consular of the Week **twice**.
    
- Can be awarded after obtaining any Tier II medal.
    
- Can be awarded for other reasons by a Sage +
    

---

**Master Endorsements:**

These endorsements can only be awarded by a Jedi Master +  
These can substitute 2 pathway endorsements (if from a Master in the path)

- Win the title of [[Consular of the Week]].
    
- Win the title of [[Staff of the Week]].
    
- Can be awarded as an additional award for some T3 medals
    
- Can be awarded for other reasons by a CM+.
    

---

**Council+ Endorsements:**  
These endorsements are a higher honor to receive, and may be required for some trials. They can only be awarded by a member of the Jedi Council.

- Can be awarded as an additional award for some T3 medals
    
- Can be awarded for other reasons by CoFK+.
    

---

**High Council+ Endorsements:**

These endorsements are the most prestigious honour a CoFK member can receive, and serve as a acknowledgement of continuous hard work or great achievements. They can only be awarded by a member of the Jedi High Council.

- Win the title of [[Scholar of the Month]].
    
- Can be awarded as an additional award to the Insignia of the Council of First Knowledge.
